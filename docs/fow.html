Here's what our seniors say about FOW!

Definitely would recommend incoming freshies to join FOW as you get to make friends from the same faculty/ course which really benefitted me in the academic year. I have some friends from FOW who are my good friends now, who are essential to help you cope with the immense stress from all the tough mods! Sean Lee Yi Ren, Y1 Business Analytics

FOW was a fun and essential transition into university life as it served as a platform for me to make my first friends in uni, and the fact that they were all in the same faculty as me meant that I had friends to plan mods and share notes with when the semester started! This made me feel blessed to have joined FOW as university life is so different from school life in jc; I would have been a lost sheep if not for the friends forged and the experiences I had which eased me into NUS life and culture. Fiona Cheong Wan Ting, Y1 Business Analytics

FOW helped me to meet new different people from all walks of life and enable me to open my circle to make new friends. Without FOW I will be lonely and nobody can guide me to adapt to University life. Thanks to FOW I am able to be more confident and open to the people around me. Marcus Foo Chuan Yao, Y1 Information Systems

FOW was a great and memorable stepping stone for me in my transition into university life. Through FOW, I met friends that would stick with me through my semesters, sharing the many highs and lows of university life. I feel fortunate to have met such lasting friends through FOW and I am sure many others feels the same way too. Our OGLs apart from giving us a memoriable camp, also gave us useful advice which has helped me through my semesters. Without FOW, my experience in university would have definitely not been as enjoyable as it has been for me. I strongly urge all incoming freshies to join FOW as it is an experience like none other you will have in your 4 years in NUS. Chew Hoa Shen, Y1 Computer Science
