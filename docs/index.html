Frequently Asked Questions!
1. When will I receive confirmation?
Emails will be sent out to accepted applicants at least one week before the event.
2. How do I make payment?
E-FOP is 100% FREE-OF-CHARGE.
3. When will I receive details of the event?
You can look forward to receiving an email regarding the details of the event (e.g. schedule) at least one week before the event!
4. Is there a deadline for registration?
There is no deadline for registration. Registration for the limited slots is primarily based on a first-come-first-serve basis so make sure to register as soon as possible!
5. What is E-FOP like?
E-FOP is a day-filled event with an exciting line-up of activities planned out for you to build long-lasting friendships with your peers and seniors, as well as to help you integrate well into the School of Computing community.
6. What happened to FSC, FOW, RAG and BASH?
Unfortunately, due to the COVID-19 situation, we are unable to execute these events this year.
7. Who can I reach out to for enquiries?
Contact us via instagram @socfop or send an email to fopenquiries@nuscomputing.com
