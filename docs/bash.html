GEEK ON FLEEK
21 Aug 2020
Get ready to party before school starts! BASH marks the finale and an opportunity for you to have fun with your friends. Conclude your FOP with a blast as the new SoC King and Queen would be crowned at the end of Bash. Stick around for the afterparty as the real fun has just begun!
Watch as your friends showcase their talent and the performance that they have specially prepared. They will be sure to surprise the crowd with their performances in a night that you won't forget!
It is also definitely an opportunity for you to gather and catch up with your new university friends after camp in an unforgettable night like this!
