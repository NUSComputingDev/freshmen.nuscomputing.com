Celebrate

Bash

24 August

Get turnt up and get ready to party! BASH marks the finale of Freshmen Orientation Projects where you get to spend the night celebrating and partying at a club with your new university friends, both Freshmen and Seniors alike. Enjoy the night as BASH candidates from FSC and FOW showcase their personalities and talents through exciting performances, along with music and games that are sure to keep you entertained through the night! Stand to walk away with exciting prizes in the lucky draw, as well as unforgettable memories of a night that is sure to be lit! Conclude your FOP with a blast as the new SoC King and Queen will be crowned at the end of BASH. Stick around for the afterparty as the real fun has only just begun! See you there!

I don’t like clubbing, really. But BASH? It’s a game changer. Have you ever walked into a club where everyone knows everyone? Yeah, me neither until BASH. The ambience was warm and comfortable; surrounded on all sides by friends, everybody was able to just be themselves, go all out and have fun! There were drinks, sick music, drinks, cool lights flashing around, more drinks. 11/10 would recommend and I’ll see you at 2018’s BASH!

What your seniors have to say about Bash!

As a freshman, BASH was a great way to kickstart my uni life - music, dance, friends and fun!! It was also a great way to wrap up my orientation with fellow students of SoC as I got to see my friends having fun performing on stage! You'll FOMO (Fear of Missing Out) if you miss it!

Teh Wen Yi, Year 1 Computer Science Student

 BASH was the ultimate finale before the official start of my academic journey in NUS School of Computing. It's the perfect opportunity to get together with all the friends I made during FSC and FOW, and to relive those moments in a night of dancing and partying. I will never forget seeing the fun side of everybody I knew, including the entertaining pageant performances. It's a night to be remembered, so get your tickets!

Cheng Xian Hao, Year 1 Computer Engineering Student
I was very impressed by the high level performances from the contestants. I never knew they were so talented! Coupled with the company of my friends who were also supporting the event, we had a very fun and entertaining night. It was an eye-opener and I would totally encourage freshmen to go and support their friends participating in BASH because not only is it a good opportunity to bond with your orientation batchmates, you get to see things you never knew the contestants were capable of!

Low Jun Wei, Year 1 Computer Engineering Student

 As a freshman going for the camps, I felt like my orientation didn't have a proper ending. But that was only until BASH! Music, dance, performances, and just having a great time with friends! Felt like the best way to wrap up freshmen orientation. It is where all friendships made in camps solidify!

Siew Qing Yuan, Year 1 Computer Science Student
