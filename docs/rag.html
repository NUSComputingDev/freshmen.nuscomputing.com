RAG
19 June - 8 August 2020
Have you ever wanted to be part of a performance for a better cause? Look no further as SoC Receive-And-Give (RAG) invites you to join us on a two month journey. In order to show appreciation to our public for their support and generous donations to our beneficiaries during FLAG, students from SoC RAG put together the performance of the summer.
Everything starts from scratch, including the Float props, Makeup and Costume designs and Dance choreograph.
Face paint is iconic for the RAG performance!
Let your creativity spill while you're designing costumes from raw materials!
No prior experience is required - You'll be learning from the best here in SoC RAG! All freshmen and seniors are encouraged to join SoC RAG to not only share their talents, but also to forge long-lasting friendships and create awesome memories.
Together, let's carve another milestone for SoC RAG. Let's go COM RAG!
Here's what our seniors say about RAG!

COM RAG has been an unforgettable experience that helped me make memories that I’ll hold close to my heart for a very long time. The dance practices and time spent making floats and costumes gave us opportunities to make new friends before the school term starts. RAG also helped to build a strong sense of belonging in SoC which makes school a little brighter during the school term. I thoroughly enjoyed my rag experience and I’m sure you will too!!! Ang Jamais, Year 1 Business Analytics

RAG was truly such a fulfilling and enjoyable experience — it was a wonderful opportunity for me to step out of my comfort zone and learn something new like dance! Furthermore, I got to interact and forge new friendships with more people from my faculty, in fact some of my closest friends were found through RAG! If you’re feeling apprehensive about entering uni life, RAG will help you make new friends and get more acquainted with the school and people! Join RAG for everlasting memories and amazing fun :-) Mavis Kwa, Year 1 Information Systems

I joined rag to meet friends before school started and I definitely didn't regret it. Rag 2018 was fun and exciting, and I enjoyed all our late night shenanigans during overnight sessions, getting to know friends and seniors better while working and talking, sharing jokes and blasting music. The friends that I have made have stayed with me even past RAG, and as an introverted person, I am forever grateful for this opportunity to meet others in a casual, low-stress environment while working on a project that has brought me great fulfilment and sense of achievement. Thng Yao Jie, Year 1 Computer Science

RAG was very tiring. But indeed, if it wasn’t tiring, it would not be fun. The friendship forged and opportunities to mingle around makes joining RAG totally worth it. Interaction between different courses are, in fact, limited during school term, and RAG serves as a great platform for you to know others in SoC. If you are one of those shy ones, RAG would be a great way to get your university started. No RAGrets Pei Yan Bo, Year 1 Computer Engineering
