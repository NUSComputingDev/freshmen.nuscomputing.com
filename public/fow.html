Freshmen Orientation Week

3 to 6 August

Freshmen of Computing, heed the call,
At Computing's orientation week, Olympus shall fall.
Seniors and Strangers shall one meet,
To make one's orientation journey truly complete.
Fun and laughter shall spread like epidemic,
Coupled with a light touch of academics.
In 4D3N shall we instill the element of amity,
To integrate everyone into the SoC family.

What your seniors have to say about FOW!

I had a blast at FOW! It's a fantastic way to meet future course mates and seniors, who to this day continue to help me with my studies. Not to mention all the fun that I had was a great way to start my university life. It is definitely an invaluable experience for any incoming freshmen.

Zachary Tang, Year 1 Computer Science Student

 FOW was amazing! It wasn't just fun and games as it helped me to kickstart my university life surrounded by my course mates and seniors who are, till this day, great friends that help one another in their studies. All in all, it’s a great way to integrate into the university life and a valuable experience that every freshman should enjoy.

Royce, Year 1 Computer Engineering Student
During my freshman year, Freshmen Orientation Week allowed me to make friends with other freshmen and seniors. This greatly benefited me when the semester started, as we helped each other with our modules.

Tzer Bin, Year 1 Computer Science Student

 FOW splits the freshmen up into groups with the same major, so it was a really great way to make friends that can become project group members in the future or friends that just help each other out. Besides that, the camp lets you know the school facilities better and you can ask your friendly orientation leaders anything regarding school life. Anyway, it's a great time and I highly recommend it!

Natania, Year 1 Information Systems Student
