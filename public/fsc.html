Socialize

Freshmen Social Camp

11 to 14 June

Wet games! Beach day! Sleepovers! Want to get a close circle of university friends before university even starts? Longing to be a part of a close knit community but don’t know where to start? Then FSC is the event for you! Meet freshmen and seniors from all the SoC majors through 4 days of exciting activities! Share moments with one another, create precious memories together and indulge yourself in fun and laughter. All fun, no academics!
