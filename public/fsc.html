Socialize

Freshmen Social Camp

11 to 14 June

Beach day! Amazing race! Sleepovers! Want to form a close circle of university friends before the semester even starts? Longing to be a part of a tightly knit community but don’t know where to begin? If so, Freshmen Social Camp is the event for you! From games likeLepak LahtoChiong Ah!, meet fellow freshmen and seniors from all the SoC majors through 4 days of exciting activities. Share moments with one another, create precious memories together and indulge yourself in tons of laughter. All fun, no academics!

Don’t waste your time at home! Sign up now, and you’ll never walk alone!

What your seniors have to say about FSC!

Going for FSC was the best choice I made before going to university because I met many people there who became my close friends that I study and have fun with. FSC is really a camp of exciting and enjoyable activities and is definitely an unforgettable one!

Vanessa Loh, Year 1 Information Systems Student

 My FSC experience was a great one! FSC is one of the rare opportunities throughout university life to make friends across different majors! This was where I was also able to get useful academic advice early on!

Francis Lee, Year 1 Computer Engineering Student
My time during FSC was great because I managed to meet many seniors who till now are guiding me in school. Not to mention the friends you'll make even before school starts, definitely one of the most important events for a freshman, the friends I made during social camp have stuck with me till today.

Darryl Leong, Year 1 Information Systems Student

 Signing up for FSC was definitely the best choice I've made in NUS! The friends I made from the camp are now my closest friends in school. Even after two years, we still look back and think about the remarkably fun times we shared during FSC. Before the camp, I was clueless about all university-related things, but my Orientation Group Leaders helped change that! FSC was not only the first but also undoubtedly the best experience I've had so far!

Low Yong Siang, Year 2 Computer Science Student
