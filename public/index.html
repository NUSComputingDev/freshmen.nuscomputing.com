Are you an incoming freshman who wants to find out more about the school? Or do you simply want to make some friends and have fun?

NUS Computing Freshmen Orientation Projects (FOP) 2017 presents to you: Tempus Nova. With an exciting lineup of events, we will help integrate into our Computing family in no time. At FOP, not only do you get the opportunity to meet other incoming freshmen, you will also meet seniors who can provide valuable advice to follow you through your University years.
