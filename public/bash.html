Bash

18 August

It's time to party! BASH marks the final event of FOP where you get to celebrate and party at a club with your new university friends. Enjoy a night of spectacular performances put up by our pageant candidates. The new Mr and Ms SoC will be crowned on the very same night! Join in on the fun and games and celebrate the night away. Did we mention that there's afterparty?

See you on 18 August at f. Club Singapore! Cheers!

What your seniors have to say about Bash!
